Prism

A beam of white light hits a glass prism ▲ and splits into red, green and blue. The apex points where the green ray continues; red and blue fan out to its sides.

Goal: steer every colour to the diamond pane that wants it — each pane is tinted and lettered (R/G/B/Y/M/C/W) with its target colour.

  • Tap a mirror to flip it /↔\; tap a prism to spin it a quarter turn. The light re-routes instantly.
  • Colours mix. Cross two beams at a pane for a secondary (R+G = yellow). Feed all three primaries back into a reversed prism to rebuild white.
  • Hard: pre-cut optics. Tray pieces have their angle already cut — a mounted piece can't rotate. Tap one, then tap the empty socket ▢ where that angle works (1 move). Tapping a mounted piece lifts it back, free.
  • Dim pieces are fixed; as panes settle, the optics feeding them frost over — that part is done.
  • Every puzzle has one solution. Beat par for a clean solve.
